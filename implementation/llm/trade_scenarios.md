# RemDarwin Component 3: Real-World Trade Scenarios for LLM Analysis

This document outlines 15+ real-world trade scenarios to validate the LLM interpretive layer for covered calls and cash-secured puts. Each scenario covers different market conditions, catalysts, and risk factors to ensure comprehensive coverage of trading situations.

## Scenario 1: Bull Market Momentum Play
**Market Conditions:** Strong uptrend, S&P 500 up 15% YTD, low volatility (VIX < 15)
**Catalyst:** Tech sector rotation with AI adoption stories
**Risk Factors:** Overvaluation concerns, potential Federal Reserve rate hike
**Trade Type:** Covered Call - Sell OTM calls to collect premium while holding stock
**Expected LLM Analysis:** Focus on momentum continuation vs. valuation risks

## Scenario 2: Bear Market Protection
**Market Conditions:** Downtrend with -8% month-over-month, elevated volatility (VIX > 25)
**Catalyst:** Recession fears, rising Treasury yields
**Risk Factors:** Further downside risk, margin calls, forced selling
**Trade Type:** Cash-Secured Put - Buy puts for protection or collect premium on dips
**Expected LLM Analysis:** Emphasize defensive positioning and risk management

## Scenario 3: Pre-Earnings Volatility Spike

## Validation Framework
Each scenario should be tested against:
- Historical precedent analysis
- Risk-reward ratio assessment
- Market timing considerations

## LLM Prompt Requirements
For each scenario, the LLM should provide:
- Trade rationale with specific catalysts
- Risk identification and mitigation
- Scenario modeling (base/upside/downside cases)
- Comparative analysis vs. alternatives
- Exit recommendations with trigger conditions
